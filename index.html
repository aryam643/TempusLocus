A Venue Booking System for a university is a web-based application that enables students, faculty, and staff to reserve various university venues such as classrooms, lecture halls, seminar rooms, sports fields, auditoriums, and other campus facilities. It streamlines the process of booking venues for various academic, administrative, and co-curricular activities such as lectures, workshops, meetings, conferences, seminars, sports events, and cultural programs.
